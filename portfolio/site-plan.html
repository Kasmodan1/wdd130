Home page

Welcome to my portfolio website! As an experienced Network Technician, I have had the opportunity to work in many different areas of information technology, gaining invaluable knowledge and expertise along the way. Through this website, I aim to share my passion for IT with you and demonstrate how my skills can help you solve complex technical challenges. On this site, you will find information about my background, including my education, certifications, and work experience. Additionally, I have included details about my specific areas of expertise, which include network design and implementation, hardware and software troubleshooting, cybersecurity, and more. As you browse through my portfolio, you will see examples of my past work. I take great pride in delivering high-quality results for my clients, and I am confident that these examples will give you a good sense of the value I can bring to your business or home environment. If you're interested in learning more about my services, you can click on the services link or if you want to know more about me click there. If you have a specific IT challenge that you need help with, I encourage you to get in touch with me. I am always open to new opportunities and would be happy to discuss your needs in more detail. You can reach me through the contact form on this website, or by phone or email. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to hearing from you soon!

About Me

Welcome to my "About Me" page! My name is Scott Midkiff, and I am a Network Technician based in Grants Pass, Oregon. Located in the beautiful Pacific Northwest, Grants Pass is a wonderful place to call home. With its stunning natural landscapes, friendly community, and abundant recreational opportunities, I feel fortunate to live and work in this amazing part of the country. When I'm not busy working on IT projects, you can often find me playing the French horn. Music has always been a big part of my life, and I've been playing the horn since I was a teenager. I love the challenge of mastering a new piece of music and the feeling of accomplishment that comes with performing in front of an audience. Whether I'm playing in a community concert or practicing at home, the French horn is a constant source of joy and inspiration in my life. I believe that my passion for music has helped me develop a strong work ethic and attention to detail, both of which are crucial in the world of IT. As a Network Technician, I understand the importance of precision and accuracy, and I am committed to delivering the best possible results for my clients. Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me. If you have any questions or would like to know more, please don't hesitate to get in touch!

Services

Welcome to my "Services" page! As an experienced Network Technician, I have worked with a wide variety of IT equipment and systems, including servers, switches, and routers. Whether you need help setting up a new network or troubleshooting an existing one, I have the skills and knowledge to get the job done quickly and efficiently. In addition to my expertise in network design and implementation, I also specialize in building custom gaming PCs for individuals. With a deep understanding of the latest hardware and software technologies, I can help you build a PC that meets your specific needs and budget. From selecting the right components to installing the latest drivers and software, I will work with you every step of the way to ensure that your gaming PC is running at peak performance. Some of the specific IT services I offer include: Network design and implementation Server installation and configuration Switch and router setup and configuration Hardware and software troubleshooting Cybersecurity assessments and solutions Gaming PC builds and upgrades No matter what your IT needs are, I am committed to providing you with the highest level of service and expertise. If you have any questions or would like to discuss a project in more detail, please don't hesitate to get in touch. I look forward to working with you!
